Field Trips

Experience real-world learning through exciting field trips and industry visits.

About Our Field Trips

Our CTE programs offer students the opportunity to participate in a variety of field trips that connect classroom learning to real-world experiences. These trips are designed to inspire, educate, and provide valuable insights into future careers.

  • Industry Tours: Visit local businesses, manufacturers, and service providers to see professionals in action.
  • College Visits: Explore technical colleges and our local community college Mt. San Jacinto Community College (MSJC)
  • Annual Carshow: Not a fieldtrip, we host an annual car show for the entire community to take part in.
  • Competitions & Events: There are times we are invited to bring a small group of students to participate in competitions (such as the UTI Top Tech Challenge).

Benefits of Field Trips

  • Gain practical knowledge on
  • Network with professionals and peers
  • Explore career and education options
  • Develop leadership and teamwork skills
  • Enhance classroom learning with real-world context
