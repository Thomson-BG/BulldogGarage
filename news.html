Rapid Evolvement

The Evolving Automotive Industry: A Technician's Guide to Thriving in a Dynamic Sector

  • Author: Josh Thomson - Aug 2, 2025

    • The automotive industry is undergoing rapid transformation, with dealership technicians needing to master new technologies like electric vehicles, ADAS, and data-driven diagnostics, shifting away from traditional mechanical skills. This evolution requires a strong focus on electronics, software, and data analysis, as well as continuous skill development. The industry is also challenged by a significant talent and skills gap, largely due to an aging workforce and fewer new entrants into automotive training programs. To succeed, technicians must embrace lifelong learning, specialize in high-demand areas, and develop both technical expertise and strong soft skills. Dealerships that invest in vocational training, career development, and foster a supportive culture will be best positioned to attract and retain skilled technicians, ensuring success in this dynamic environment.

    Vehicle Maintenance

    Tire Technology: The Science and Sustainability of Modern Tires

  • Author: Josh Thomson - Aug 2, 2025

    • Tires are highly engineered products made from a blend of specialized materials like different rubbers, carbon black, and silica, which are shaped, assembled, and vulcanized into durable, high-performance components. Innovations in material science, such as the use of silica compounds, sustainable resources, and nanotechnology, are improving grip, durability, and fuel efficiency while reducing environmental impact. Technologies like smart tires and low rolling resistance designs are enhancing vehicle safety, energy efficiency, and adaptability, especially important for electric and autonomous vehicles. The tire industry faces environmental challenges throughout a tire's lifecycle, but sustainable practices—like recycled materials, energy-efficient production, retreading, and advanced recycling methods—are being adopted to mitigate these issues. Strict safety regulations and proper tire selection and maintenance remain crucial for ensuring optimal vehicle performance and road safety in various driving conditions.
