Ford Automotive Career Exploration

Ford ACE

Get certified in Ford technologies and gain dealership-level experience.

Why Choose Ford ACE?

  • Industry-Recognized Credentials: Students can earn Ford Service Technician Specialty Training (STST) certifications, making them highly employable after graduation.
  • Real-World Experience: The program includes hands-on labs, access to modern diagnostic tools, and exposure to the latest Ford vehicle technology.
  • Dealership Connections: ACE students are often prioritized for internships and job shadowing at local Ford and Lincoln dealerships.
  • College & Career Pathways: ACE is a stepping stone to post-secondary automotive programs and direct-to-work opportunities.
  • Flexible Learning: Online modules allow students to learn at their own pace, with support from instructors and Ford professionals.
“The Ford ACE program gave me a head start in my automotive career. I graduated with certifications that helped me get hired at a Ford dealership right out of high school!”
— Former ACE Student

What is the Ford ACE Program?

The Ford Automotive Career Exploration (ACE) program is a partnership between Ford Motor Company, Ford/Lincoln dealerships, and high school or post-secondary institutions. It provides students with access to Ford's web-based training, the same as dealership technicians, and helps prepare them for a career in the automotive industry.

  • Earn Ford certifications while in high school
  • Access to Ford's online technical training modules
  • Hands-on experience with modern vehicles and technology
  • Connect with local Ford/Lincoln dealerships for internships and job opportunities
  • Stand out for future automotive careers or post-secondary programs

Learn more or sign in at highschool.fordservicetraining.com/signin
