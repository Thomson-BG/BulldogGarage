Club Activities

Weekly Meetings

Fridays 3:30 PM

Regular club meetings to discuss projects, plan events, and share automotive knowledge and experiences.

  • Technical discussions
  • Project planning
  • Guest speakers
  • Industry updates

Restoration Projects

Ongoing

Long-term vehicle restoration projects where club members work together to bring classic cars back to life.

  • Engine rebuilding
  • Body work and paint
  • Interior restoration
  • Modern upgrades

Field Trips

Monthly

Educational visits to automotive facilities, museums, and industry events to expand learning beyond the classroom.

  • Dealership tours
  • Auto shows
  • Racing events
  • Manufacturing plants

Skills Competitions

Seasonal

Participate in regional and state-level automotive technology competitions and skills challenges.

  • SkillsUSA events
  • Diagnostic challenges
  • Speed competitions
  • Team challenges

Join Bulldog Garage Club

Ready to take your automotive passion to the next level? Join our club and become part of a community dedicated to excellence in automotive education.

Membership Requirements

  • Currently enrolled in Bulldog Garage automotive program
  • Maintain minimum 2.5 GPA
  • Commit to regular meeting attendance
  • Participate in at least 2 club activities per semester
  • Demonstrate positive attitude and teamwork

Member Benefits

  • Priority access to advanced equipment
  • Exclusive workshops and training sessions
  • Industry networking opportunities
  • Competition team eligibility
  • Club merchandise and recognition
