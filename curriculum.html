Automotive Curriculum

Comprehensive 4-year program designed to prepare students for automotive industry careers

Program Overview

Our four-year automotive program provides students with comprehensive training in automotive technology, from fundamental concepts to advanced systems. Each year builds upon the previous, ensuring students develop both theoretical knowledge and practical skills essential for success in the automotive industry.

01

Foundation Year

Introduction to automotive fundamentals, safety procedures, and basic maintenance operations.

  • Shop Safety & Tool Usage
  • Basic Engine Components
  • Fluid Systems Overview
  • Tire & Wheel Service
  • Basic Electrical Concepts
View Details
02

Intermediate Skills

Building on fundamentals with engine systems, braking, and suspension technologies.

  • Engine Performance Systems
  • Brake System Service
  • Suspension & Steering
  • Cooling & Heating Systems
  • Electrical Diagnostics
View Details
03

Advanced Systems

Advanced diagnostics, computer systems, and specialized automotive technologies.

  • Computer Diagnostics
  • Fuel Injection Systems
  • Transmission Service
  • Air Conditioning Systems
  • Advanced Electrical
View Details
04

Professional Preparation

Industry certifications, hybrid/electric vehicles, and career preparation for professional entry.

  • ASE Certification Prep
  • Hybrid & Electric Vehicles
  • Advanced Diagnostics
  • Shop Management
  • Career Portfolio
View Details

Program Features

Industry Certifications

Students prepare for and earn industry-recognized certifications including ASE credentials and manufacturer-specific training.

Modern Technology

Training on the latest diagnostic equipment, computer systems, and emerging automotive technologies including electric vehicles.

Industry Partnerships

Collaborations with local dealerships and repair facilities provide real-world experience and job placement opportunities.

Skills Competitions

Opportunities to compete in regional and national automotive technology competitions through SkillsUSA and other organizations.

Career Pathways

Automotive Technician

Entry-level positions in dealerships, independent repair shops, and specialty automotive services.

Starting: $35,000 - $45,000

Diagnostic Specialist

Advanced technical positions focusing on complex automotive diagnostic and repair procedures.

Experienced: $50,000 - $70,000

Electric Vehicle Technician

Specialized roles in the growing electric vehicle market, requiring advanced electrical systems knowledge.

Specialized: $60,000 - $80,000

Service Manager

Management positions overseeing automotive service operations and leading technical teams.

Management: $70,000 - $100,000+

Ready to Start Your Automotive Career?

Join our comprehensive automotive program and gain the skills needed for a successful career in the automotive industry.

Admission Requirements

  • Current enrollment at Hemet High School
  • Interest in automotive technology and careers
  • Commitment to safety and professional development
  • Willingness to learn and work in team environment

Program Information

  • Duration: 4-year program
  • Class Size: Limited to 20 students per class
  • Schedule: Available during regular school hours
  • Prerequisites: Basic math and reading skills
